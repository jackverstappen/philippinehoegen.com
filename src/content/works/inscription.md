*Inscription  
*  
Philippine Hoegen and Carolien Stikker  
**September 19 - November 29 2009  
  
Inscription was an artist-residency-based commissioned exhibition by Amsterdam-based artists Philippine Hoegen and Carolien Stikker at the Samuel Dorsky Museum, New Paltz, New York.**

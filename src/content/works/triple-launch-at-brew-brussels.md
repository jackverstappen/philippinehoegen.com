*Triple Launch at BREW Brussels: M. Arkesteyn, S. Reuzé and V.de Gruyter  
  
*Our first event at BREW*: a triple launch, on Saturday June 1st, 6 to 8pm. Three new releases will each be introduced in a short presentation, the talks and discussion will be in English.  
  
 PUBLICATION—  
 When the Second Voice Does Not Occupy a Stable Position / Het Verband Tussen Verlangen en Controleverlies, Controleverlies als Vermogen en Vorm van Inzicht by MADDY ARKESTEYN  
 Calcite Revolt, Amsterdam 2012 isbn 9789081968003 / [www.calciterevolt.com](http://www.calciterevolt.com/)   
 With texs by Ilse van Rijn and Anna Luyten. Design: Hansje van Halem 140 and 48 p, ills colour, 13 x 24 and 17 x 24 cm  
 PRESENTATION by MARC VAN DEN BOSSCHE, professor of philosophy Vrije Universiteit Brussel  
   
 EDITION—  
 Brooklands  
 by SÉBASTIEN REUZÉ  
 Lauréat du programme "Hors les murs" de l'Institut Français, [w](http://goog_1725283648/)[ww.institutfrancais.com](http://ww.institutfrancais.com/)[  
 Case](http://goog_1725283648/) 29,7 x 43 cm,15 inkjet prints A3  
 1 video : Brooklands (20 mn)  
 Edition of 15, 2012  
 PRESENTATION by SÉBASTIEN REUZÉ  
  
 PUBLICATION—  
 From Action of Matchmaking Photons to Zen Buddhists’ Bad Breath by VOEBE DE GRUYTER   
 Roma Publications, Amsterdam 2012 isbn 9789077459874 / [www.romapublications.org  
 With](http://www.romapublications.org/) texts by Maria Barnas and Roel Arkesteijn, plus explanatory notes for almost all of the works. Design: Felix Weigand 216 p, ills colour & bw, 24 x 34 cm, pb, English PRESENTATION by VOEBE DE GRUYTER in converstaion with Philippine Hoegen  
  
 **Saturday June 1st, 18.00 to 20.00 hrs  
 ****with drinks and snacks   
 ****BREW****, Schootstraat 1 Rue du Pene, 1000 Brussels  
 ****Metro Comte de Flandre/Graaf van Vlaanderen or Sainte-Catherine/Sint-Katelijne
